Mission

Our mission at BentoBoxes is to revolutionize the way people experience and enjoy meals by providing convenient, healthy, and customizable meal solutions in the form of bento boxes. We aim to offer a diverse range of nutritious and delicious options that cater to various dietary preferences and lifestyles. Our mission is to empower individuals to make better food choices, improve their overall well-being, and enhance their productivity and satisfaction through a balanced and mindful approach to eating.

Vission

At BentoBoxes, our vision is to become the go-to brand for premium, personalized, and sustainable meal solutions. We envision a world where everyone has access to wholesome and satisfying meals that are thoughtfully crafted to meet their unique needs. We strive to be at the forefront of innovation, continually developing new and exciting flavors, while ensuring our offerings are environmentally conscious and promote a healthy lifestyle. Through our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we aspire to inspire and transform the way people perceive and enjoy their meals, making BentoBoxes a trusted and beloved brand worldwide.

Core Values

Our core values at BentoBoxes are quality, health and well-being, customization, sustainability, innovation, and customer focus.

HISTORY

Bento boxes have a rich history that dates back centuries in Japan. The concept of a bento, meaning "convenient" or "handy," originated during the Kamakura period (1185-1333) as a simple meal packed for travelers or field workers. However, the bento as we know it today has evolved over time.
